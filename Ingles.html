REGULAS VERBS

Regular verbs in English are those that only need to add the ending “ed” to form their past tense and participle. This means that to form any tense and person, a specific morpheme is added to the lexeme of the verb.

IRREGULAR VERBS

Irregular verbs are verbs that have particular or idiosyncratic conjugations depending on the time or way in which they are conjugated. It experiences changes in terms of the paradigm, since it takes different endings than those that are common in regular verbs.

ADJECTIVES

Adjectives are words that accompany a noun or noun, giving us information about it and completing its meaning. That is, the adjectives that help us describe the characteristics of things. Ex. The big house; the black cat; Ana is thinner than me.

ADVERBS

Adverbs serve to modify or specify the meaning of other words. They usually modify verbs, telling us how, how often, when or where something happens. The adverb comes after the verb it modifies. They really have so much power that they can change the meaning of an entire sentence.

ADJETIVOS:

Los adjetivos son palabras que acompañan a un nombre o sustantivo,dándonos información sobre el mismo y completando su significado. Es decir, los adjetivos nos sirven para describir las características de las cosas. Ej. La casa grande ; el gato negro ; Ana es más delgada que yo.

ADVRBIOS:

Los adverbios sirven para modificar o precisar el significado de otras palabras. Normalmente modifican verbos, diciéndonos cómo, con qué frecuencia, cuándo, o dónde sucede algo. El adverbio va detrás del verbo que modifica.Realmente tienen tanto poder, que pueden cambiar el significado de una oración entera
